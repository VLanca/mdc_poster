Summary
The idea of the project will focus on the Open Source theme. Based on the Do It Yourself concept, users or organizations can benefit from the use of a design, product, tools or service, in which they have an active role in the development of new Open Source resources. This is formed by communities, in which employees share and make it available for anyone to use them freely. This project will have an approach to ideological aspects, ethics and access to tools. This project will have the intention of introducing / introducing a guide to Open Source practices, adding a mapping of concepts and references.
